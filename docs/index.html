About This Tool

15 Governance Controls

Comprehensive coverage of AI governance requirements including accountability, data governance, model documentation, bias testing, and human oversight.

NIST AI RMF Aligned

Controls are mapped to NIST AI Risk Management Framework functions: GOVERN, MAP, MEASURE, and MANAGE.

EU AI Act Ready

Risk tier classification and compliance checking aligned with EU AI Act requirements for high-risk AI systems.

Actionable Guidance

Failed controls include specific remediation steps and required artifacts to help you achieve compliance.
